What is MoviFeree?

MoviFeree is a Website That Helps you to find a movie to watch.

How much does MovieFeree cost?

MoviFeree is completely free.

How does MoviFeree work?

All you have to do is Answer a couple questions, and based on your answers we will recommend a movie to you!

What inspired MoviFeree?

There are many people at home right now due to Covid, therefore they are home probably watching movies daily. We want to build an application where people at home can take a quick quiz to suggest/recommend some movies that may be interesting for them to watch at home!
